About Us

Built by trainers, run for learners.

CADD Expert Training Institution is a design, IT and business training centre based in Tenkasi, focused on hands-on, project-based learning — not just lectures.

Who We Are

A training institution grounded in real work

CADD Expert Training Institution trains students, graduates and working professionals across Graphic Design, Web & Software Development, Digital Marketing, Business Tools, Testing, App Development and Trending Technologies. Every course pairs software skills with real-time projects, so what you learn in class becomes something you can show an employer.

We keep batches small with an individual system and an individual trainer for every learner, run classes online, offline, on weekends or in fast-moving formats, and back every course with study material, software installation support and authorized certification.
